MAHESH BABU

Mahesh Babu is an Indian actor and producer. He is a renowned media personality in the Telugu film industry. He is a popular philanthropist.After making his debut as a lead actor in the year 1999, Mahesh Babu has been part of many successful movies and has bagged more than 15 awards in his career; popular awards include Nandi Awards, Filmfare Awards, CineMAA Awards, South Indian International Movie Awards, and International Indian Film Academy Awards. Babu married his co-star Namrata Shirodkar on 10 February 2005 at the Marriott Hotel, Mumbai during the shooting of Athadu (2005). The couple's first child was born on 31 August 2006 at the Global Hospital in Hyderabad. The boy was named Gautam. On 20 July 2012, Shirodkar gave birth to a girl whom they named Sitara.
